<Notes>
- It's just CSS so again it's cross-platform compatible
- You enforce your low-level rules of your system
- Highly predictable -> you don't spend time debugging conflicting CSS styles, what you add to your component is what you get.
- You can run into specificity issues trying to create style APIs for your components
- You should always use this with STRONG componentization or you lose consistency; developers will make judgement calls themselves between p4 or p3 in different places
</Notes>

- Any sized project
- Projects with consistent low-level design
- Teams who will make smaller components
- Fast prototyping
